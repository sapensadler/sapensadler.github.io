# JKA WF International Summer Camp — Paisley (2026)

Dates: Friday 7th – Sunday 9th August 2026

Location: Paisley, Scotland

## Instructors

- Masao Kawasoe sensei 
- Oishi Shuseki Shihan (JKA Honbu)
- Kobayashi sensei (JKA Honbu)

## Overview

This international Summer Camp brings together senior instructors from JKA Honbu and JKA WF England for three days of intensive Shotokan training. The programme includes kihon (basics), kata (forms), kumite (sparring) suitable for senior students and instructors.

### Benefits

- Intensive technical coaching from JKA Honbu and senior JKAWF instructors
- Focused kata and kumite sessions to improve precision, timing and competition readiness
- Networking with senior students and dojos across the UK and internationally

### Who should attend

- Senior kyu and dan grades, instructors and experienced karateka seeking high-level instruction
- Kawasoe group students: please book through your own club (see your instructor for registration)
- External students: welcome to attend. External students should register with event organisers where requested and may pay on the day (please bring cash ).
