# Welcome to JKA WF England

We promote and preserve authentic Shotokan karate across England as an affiliated member of the Japan Karate Association (JKA). Our mission is to teach traditional technique, develop character, and support dojos across England through training, grading and events.

- Authentic JKA Shotokan instruction
- National courses, gradings and tournaments
- A growing network of dojos across England

Under Sensei Kawasoe’s guidance, we maintain a friendly, disciplined, and supportive atmosphere where beginners and experienced karateka train side by side. Whether your goal is fitness, confidence, self-defence, or deep study of traditional karate, you’ll find a place here at JKA WF England.
